Sparticus Minecraft Client

A Minecraft Client made in Unity

Both Java and Bedrock Edition Support

Sparticus Client plans on having you connect to both Java and Bedrock edition, so you can play with your friends whichever they use!

Any Version

No longer will you have to worry about your friend who refuses to update to the latest version of the game. Sparticus Client plans to have support for versions 1.7.5 to 1.15.2 or any major release after.

Unity Game Engine

Sparticus is developed in the Unity Game Engine. This allows for support on any Operating System and control scheme. We might even have a webpage here to play it :)

PvP In Mind

Sparticus Client will be built with PvP in mind for the people who still play on Minecraft version 1.8.9

Modding Support

Sparticus will have it's own modding API! The API will have hacking/cheating in mind and will prevent things like that from happening. A converter for Forge mods to Sparticus mods might come along later down the line.

RAY TRACING!

The Sparticus Minecraft Client plans to have support for ray tracing and more graphical effects for a low performance cost!
